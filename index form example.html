From Head of School to Heart of a Movement

Donna led one of the nation's most innovative Montessori-based schools for 3 decades. Now she shares her energy and expertise with schools, nonprofits, and foundations to spark student voice, deepen belonging, and fuel transformation at scale.

Her approach combines decades of educational leadership with a deep understanding of creative learning environments, helping organizations reimagine what's possible when we put imagination and belonging at the center of everything we do.
