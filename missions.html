About the Missions

Executive Development Missions are immersive experiences designed to fundamentally shift how leaders see, think, and act. Our signature Japan Study Mission takes you to the birthplace of lean thinking, but we're open to developing custom missions around any transformational methodology, design thinking, innovation, sustainability, wherever the source of excellence can be found.

Japan Study Mission

Immerse Yourself in the Birthplace of Lean

Characteristics

  • • Training by Consultants with Toyota Motors Background
  • • Small guided groups of approximately 20 participants
  • • Daily group discussions and reflection

Objectives

  • • Study the Background and Structure of Toyota Production System
  • • Tour World's Highest Level Factories
  • • Learn Japanese Culture Behind TPS

Experience Highlights

  • • Dojo immersion with lean simulations and former Toyota executives
  • • Visit Toyota Kaikan and Toyota Tech Museum
  • • Guided by Toyota trained professionals
